Nigel Slater’s salmon macaroni recipe The recipe Pour 600ml of double cream into a saucepan, add 400g of salmon, a little black pepper and a bay leaf and bring almost to the boil. Lower the heat and let the salmon cook gently for about 10 minutes until the flesh comes apart with ease, then remove from the heat. Boil 150g of serpentelli or macaroni in generously salted water for nine minutes then drain and tip into a baking dish.
